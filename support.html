Frequently asked

My steps look wrong.

Amble reads steps from Apple Health. Check that Amble has Health access in Settings › Privacy & Security › Health › Amble, and that your iPhone is counting steps in the Health app itself.

Routes won't generate.

Route planning needs location access and an internet connection. Make sure location is allowed for Amble in system settings, then try again from an outdoor spot.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open Settings › your name › Subscriptions, select Amble, and manage or cancel there. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Profile › Delete account. This permanently removes your community account, posts, comments, followers, battles and badges.

How do I report a post or block someone?

Press and hold a post or comment to report it or block that walker. Blocked walkers disappear from your feed, and reported content is hidden once flagged.
